Little Guy Brand Guide

The concept of the brand is a cat cafe - a place where people can come to enjoy drinks and small meals in the presence of cute, friendly cats.

The fonts I am using are Martel by Dan Reynolds for the bulk of the text, and Prata by Cyreal for anything I need to be more stylized such as headers.

As for colors I am using teal for the background, alice blue for the main text, and light green for accents with the intent of making a pleasant, cool-toned environment for the user.

The mission of Little Guy Cafe is to provide joy to both humans and cats by bringing them together.
